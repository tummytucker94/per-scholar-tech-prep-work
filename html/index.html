Chocolate Fudge

Description

This is a beginner-friendly recipe for creating chocolate fudge.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chocolate chips (milk, dark, or semi-sweet)
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optional: 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Nuts, marshmallows, or other mix-ins

Steps

  1. Step 1: Line an 8 × 8 inch pan with parchment paper or lightly grease it.
  2. Step 2: Add the chocolate chips, sweetened condensed milk, and butter to a medium saucepan.
  3. Step 3: Heat on low, stirring constantly, until the mixture is fully melted and smooth.
  4. Step 4: Remove from heat and stir in vanilla, salt, and any mix-ins you like.
  5. Step 5: Pour the mixture into the prepared pan and smooth the top with a spatula.
  6. Step 6: Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until firm.
  7. Step 7: Lift the fudge out using the parchment paper, cut into squares, and enjoy.
